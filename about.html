About ProResume AI

We help job seekers create resumes that get past automated screening and into the hands of hiring managers.

Our Mission

ProResume AI was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves a fair shot at their dream job. Too many qualified candidates get filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human ever sees their resume. We built ProResume AI to level the playing field — combining professional design, ATS optimization, and AI-powered writing to help job seekers present their best selves.

What We Do

ProResume AI is a subscription-based software platform (SaaS) that provides:

  • AI-assisted resume writing and optimization
  • ATS-compatible professional resume templates
  • Job description keyword matching
  • High-quality PDF resume export
  • Resume scoring and improvement suggestions

Our service is delivered entirely online through our web platform. Customers can sign up for a free plan or subscribe to paid plans from $3–$20/month for full access to all features.
