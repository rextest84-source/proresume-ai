Frequently asked questions

What is ProResume AI?

ProResume AI is a cloud-based software service (SaaS) that helps job seekers create professional, ATS-optimized resumes using AI-powered writing tools, premium templates, and PDF export functionality.

What are AI credits?

AI credits power premium features like AI writing enhancement (2 credits), PDF export (3 credits), job matching (5 credits), and more. Free users get 10 credits. Subscriptions include monthly allowances, or buy one-time credit packs from our pricing page.

What do I get with a Pro subscription?

Pro subscribers ($15/month) receive 200 AI credits monthly, 12 premium templates, unlimited PDF exports, job description matching, cover letter generator, and priority support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings or by emailing support@proresumeai.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund. See our Refund Policy for details.

How is my data protected?

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest. We do not sell personal data. Read our full Privacy Policy.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@proresumeai.com or visit our Contact page. We respond within 1 business day.
