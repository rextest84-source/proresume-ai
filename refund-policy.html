Refund Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Our Commitment

We want you to be satisfied with ProResume AI. If our Service doesn't meet your expectations, we offer a straightforward refund policy outlined below.

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you purchase a Pro subscription and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase or upgrade to a paid plan. No questions asked.

This guarantee applies to:

  • First-time Pro subscriptions
  • Upgrades from Free to Pro
  • Annual Pro subscriptions (if offered)

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us via one of the following methods:

  • Email: support@proresumeai.com with subject line "Refund Request"
  • Contact form — select "Refund Request" as the subject

Please include the email address associated with your account. We process refund requests within 3–5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time. To cancel:

  • Go to your account settings and click "Cancel Subscription," or
  • Email support@proresumeai.com

Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will not be charged for subsequent billing periods
  • No cancellation fees apply
  • Your account reverts to the Free plan after the paid period ends

What Is Not Refundable

  • Subscription fees for billing periods that ended more than 14 days ago
  • Partial-month refunds after the 14-day guarantee period (cancellation stops future charges but does not refund the current period)
  • Enterprise plans with custom contracts (governed by individual agreements)

Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We are committed to resolving issues quickly. Unauthorized or fraudulent chargebacks may result in account suspension.

Free Plan

The Free plan is provided at no cost and does not involve any charges. No refund applies as no payment is made.
